Gumbo

Indulge in the irresistible flavors of Louisiana with a steaming bowl of gumbo. This iconic Creole dish boasts tender meats or seafood, savory veggies, and a silky okra roux. Enhanced with a blend of spices, gumbo is the perfect comfort food to enjoy over a bed of fluffy rice. Follow our recipe for an authentic taste of the South.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 ounces)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons Creole seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup sliced okra
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the flour and stir constantly for 15-20 minutes, or until the roux is a dark brown color.
  2. Add the onions, bell pepper, and celery to the pot and stir well. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the garlic and sausage to the pot and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the sausage is browned.
  4. Add the chicken to the pot and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, bay leaves, Creole seasoning, thyme, oregano, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Add the shrimp and okra to the pot and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  8. Remove the bay leaves from the pot and season the gumbo with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve the gumbo over cooked rice.

Enjoy your delicious Gumbo!
