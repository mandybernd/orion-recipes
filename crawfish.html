Crawfish Étouffée

Satisfy your taste buds with the rich and spicy flavors of crawfish étouffée. This classic Cajun dish features tender crawfish tails smothered in a roux-based sauce, seasoned with a blend of spices like cayenne pepper and paprika. Enjoy it over a bed of fluffy rice for the ultimate comfort food experience. Follow our recipe below to recreate this Southern favorite in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. crawfish tails
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 cups seafood or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp. Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hot cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add flour and stir constantly until mixture turns a caramel color.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic to the pot and cook for 5 minutes, or until vegetables are soft.
  3. Add crawfish tails and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Slowly pour in broth, stirring constantly until mixture comes to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add green onions, parsley, Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. Serve over hot cooked rice.

Serve it over hot cooked rice for a satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Cajun country.
